1. Business Reason & Justification for the App

Bagmati Traders runs a multi-channel retail and wholesale business in Nepal, managing orders across physical storefronts and the Daraz Marketplace.

Currently, our operations suffer from manual processing delays. Operating the seller customer support chat separately from our central inventory system leads to delay in responding to customer product queries and post-order requests. Additionally, manually transcribing order details causes stock status synchronization delay.

By establishing access to the Daraz Open Platform (specifically the ERP IM Chat API), our application integrates messaging alongside inventory control:

  • Automated Order Mapping & Ingestion: Seamlessly imports orders triggered by customer checkouts directly to internal processing queues.
  • Real-time Status Tracking: Automatic logging of customer reverse orders (returns) and delivery stages.
  • Immediate Chat Assistance: Automatically checks internal live inventory databases to answer customer questions about product dimensions, stock availability, and shipping times.
  • AI and Keyword Automation: Provides swift response templates and Gemini AI assistance to resolve buyer queries within seconds, raising our seller response rate metrics on Daraz.

This is an internally used application, strictly restricted to our own merchant store accounts to improve operational standards, reduce cancellation rates, and provide high-quality support to Daraz buyers.

2. Design Documentation & Data Flows

2.1 System Architecture

The integration is designed as a hybrid cloud architecture:

  • Frontend Interface (React/Next.js): Dashboard for staff agents to track logs, chat history, and configure keyword templates.
  • Deno Edge Layer (Supabase Functions): Lightweight, zero-cold-start webhook receiver that intercepts Daraz events, verifies cryptographic payload signatures, and performs routing.
  • Database & AI Layer (PostgreSQL & Gemini): Saves message models, coordinates stock ledgers, and generates assistant answers.
